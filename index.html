Meet the Innovators Behind the Magic

This innovative smart kiosk was developed by Gopika A, Gummadi Tejaswi, Kavana B K, and Chaithra M N — final year students of Electronics and Communication Engineering. Designed to deliver institutional information seamlessly, this kiosk is adaptable to any public space, offering users a convenient and intuitive way to explore key details about their surroundings. A standout feature is the inclusion of a 3D holographic display, which brings an added layer of interactivity and creativity, making this project a blend of both technical depth and engaging design.

Smart Kiosk Overview

The Smart Kiosk is a futuristic, voice-enabled system designed to deliver essential institutional information with ease. It features a touchscreen interface paired with a microphone for voice interactions, alongside a holographic pyramid display that brings a new dimension of visual engagement. With a compact and intuitive design, it is perfect for placement in academic or public spaces, where accessibility and innovation meet.
